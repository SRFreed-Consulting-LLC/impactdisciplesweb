Approved Plan for Growing Your Church

The Mission:
The first portion of our seminar revolves around defining the mission Jesus left for us in the Great Commission: to make disciples. What is a disciple, and why do we need to make them?
The Model:
The second portion of our seminar points us toward the perfect model for disciple-makers: Jesus. We look at how He modeled disciple-making for His own disciples in His own ministry.
The Method:
The final portion of our seminar shows you the methods Jesus used to multiply disciples during His ministry on Earth and ensured that they would continue to multiply once He left.
The Takeaways:
You’ll walk away with a full understanding of the target, strategy, environments, and leadership development needed to create a sustainable disciple making ministry in your church.
